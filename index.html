Loop.Coop Present: Bemusical Chairs

An enchantingly-interactive installation blending light, colour and a pleasant sit down.

You are invited to discover this curious triad of chairs, take a seat, and experience the installation springing to life around you.

Each individual seat, when sat on, triggers a delicately beautiful animated light display. When all three chairs are occupied, the installation is fully alive as all three light displays intertwine.

When you move on, the lights fade away, awaiting the next curious sitter to bring them back to life.
